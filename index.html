BOOKS FACILITIES:


2 Books will be issued to all the students on Borrowers card for 15 days.

2 Books will be issued on the PSLL scheme for the entire year to the poor and meritorious students.

JOURNALS AND PERIODICALS FACILITY:


Journals and periodicals will be issued to the students on demand for home reading for 1-2 days on the production of identity cards.

REFERENCE SECTION:


The reference section will be kept open during library hours.

Books, journals, periodicals, and question papers from the previous year will be issued on identity cards for reference.

LIBRARY WORKING HOURS:


Generally, the library will be kept open on all working days. The reading hours of the library are ​

Monday to Friday 08.00 a.m to 06.00 p.m.
Saturday 08.00 a.m to 02.00 p.m.

A special reading room is provided during examinations from 08.00 a.m to 08.00 p.m (Except Sundays and Holidays).

USER ORIENTATION PROGRAMME:


The freshers are formally introduced to the library services and facilities at the beginning of every academic year, class-wise about books, journals, periodicals, and others.

NEW ARRIVALS DISPLAY:



Some selected titles of new books acquired by the library are displayed in the New Arrivals Display stand for the benefit of users. These books will be on display for at least one week after that they will be made available to borrow.

INTERLIBRARY LOANS:


It is not possible for any institution, however rich its resources, to buy all the books published even in a discipline. Hence it is inevitable to depend on other sister organizations to share information sources for the mutual benefit of the users.
